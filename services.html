General/Primary Services:
  • Preventive Care: routine checkups, screenings, and vaccinations to help prevent illness and diseases
  • Diagnosis and Treatment of Common Illnesses or Disease: managing everyday or ongoing health issues
  • Telehealth (Click Here to Learn More!)

Specialized Services:
  • Cardiology: heart and blood vessels related care
  • Oncology: diagnosis and treatment of cancer
  • Nephrology: kidney-related diseases care
  • Orthopedics: bones, joints, muscles and related conditions care
  • Endocrinology: hormone-related conditions care
  • Respiratory care: lung and breathing problems care
  • Nutrition care: diet and nutrition guidance for health improvement or managing diseases
  • Gynecology: women's reproductive health care
  • Immunology: immune system disorders care
  • Dermatology: skin, hair, and nails care

Dental Services:
  • Oral Hygiene: teeth cleaning, exams, and preventive dental care
  • Periodontology: gum diseases and structures care
  • Orthodontics: straightening of teeth procedure
  • Dental Implants: permanent replacement teeth procedure

Mental Health Services:
  • Therapy: conversing with a mental health professional to handle emotions and mental health issues
  • Psychiatrist: diagnosing of mental health disorders
  • Clinical Screening: tests or evaluations in order to identify mental health conditions
